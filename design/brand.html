The letter treatment

Signature element — the ember

One radial gradient per page, breathing on a 11–13 second cycle — roughly the pace of slow, calming breath. It's the only thing that moves without being asked. Nothing else animates ambiently: no parallax, no floating shapes, no confetti. The ember is the product's pulse; on dark mode it reads as banked coals.

radial-gradient · scale 1 → 1.13 · 12s ease-in-out · infinite

Voice rules

Never
“Your hormones are talking” · “Cycle syncing” · “Girl, you've got this!” · streaks, scores, red warning states · clinical jargon without consent · exclamation marks on hard days
Always
Second person, present tense · names the feeling without owning her (“the fog”, not “your fog”) · permission over instruction · exits everywhere (“skip”, “rather not say”, “maybe later”)
